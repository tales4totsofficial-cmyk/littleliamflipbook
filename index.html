Little Liam woke up with the sun so bright.
"What adventure will I have today?" he said with delight.
He grabbed his backpack and his teddy bear, Buddy too.
"Let's explore the backyard! There's so much to do!"
1
The grass was tall, like a jungle so green.
Captain Whiskers the cat said, "Follow me to be seen!"
They walked through flowers, past colors so bright.
A butterfly joined them, oh what a sight!
2
They came to a puddle, wide and blue.
Pip the Cloud giggled, "I'll carry you!"
They floated across on the cloud so light.
The puddle-ocean sparkled, shining bright!
3
Two squirrels were gathering acorns with care.
"We need help," said Zippy. "Will you share?"
Little Liam smiled and joined in the fun.
Working together, the job was soon done!
4
A big tree stood tall, like a mountain so high.
"Let's climb to the top and touch the sky!"
Step by step, up they went with care.
At the top sat Mr. Tortoise, wise and fair.
5
"Being brave means trying something new.
Take a deep breath, you know what to do!"
The sun was setting, painting the sky.
Luna the fairy lit their way home, flying by.
6
Back at the door, Liam gave hugs all around.
"Thank you for the best adventure I've found!"
His friends all waved from the garden so green.
"See you tomorrow!" called the whole team.
7
At dinner, Mom asked with care,
"What did you do today, my little bear?"
"I crossed an ocean and climbed up high!
I made new friends under the sky!"
8
Now tucked in bed, with Buddy held near,
Little Liam smiled, feeling no fear.
"Tomorrow I'll have adventures so new.
But tonight I'll dream of the ones I just knew!"
